Part I - (Dataset Exploration of The Loan Data From Prosper)¶

by (Osasenaga Oriakhi)¶

Introduction¶

This data set is called the Loan Data from Prosper.It contains 113,937 loans with 81 variables on each loan.It contains details about the borrowers' employment status,whether or not their income can be verified,whether or not they own homes,whether or not they have taken a Prosper loan before,their credit score range,their occupation,the purpose of the loan, etc. The variables also include the loan amount, borrower rate (or interest rate), current loan status, borrower income, and many others.

What is the structure of your dataset?¶

The dataset consist of 113937 entries and 81 columns, only a few of these columns will be used for this exploration and so I have dropped sme f the columns that have missing data and are not necessary for the exploration.The data contains no duplicate data and is missing a number of data of which some have been dropped.

What is/are the main feature(s) of interest in your dataset?¶

I am interested in a few things.First, whether we have more unemployed people taking the loans than employed people,also whether there are more unemployed peple defaulting on the loan payment than employed people, whether people prefer to take longer term loans than shorter term loans.Then, the most common reasons for the loan acquistion, and the relatinship between the borrower's interest rate and the borrower's annual interest rate.Then, I wanted to see the relationship between the borrower's monthly income , whether or not their income is verifiable and the employment status.

What features in the dataset do you think will help support your investigation into your feature(s) of interest?¶

The following features of the datasets will be important for this investigation EmploymentStatus, LoanStatus, Term, BarrowerRate, BorrowerAPR,LoanOriginalAmount,Listingcategory,IsBorrowerHomeowner,TotalProsperLoan,IncomeVerifiable and StatedMonthlyIncome
